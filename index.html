Cairn: Mathair's Curse

Cairn: Mathair’s Curse is a JRPG about a boy and his mother. When an ancient curse is unleashed by a mysterious cult, Alex must venture from his home, make some unlikely friends, and awaken the power of his dreams to save his family…


Features

  • Full-length single-player JRPG
  • A story-rich narrative steeped in Celtic mythology and Scottish culture
  • Use your dreams to enter a mysterious dark realm
  • Funky turned-based combat
  • Meet lots of weird and quirky characters

Meet the Team

  • Alex lives with his family but never strays too far from his mum. He is often seen roaming the island with his best friend, Andy.
  • An explorer through and through, Andy cannot pass up an adventure. One of these days, Andy's adventures could land him in hot water, or worse.
  • A natural musician, Eilidh concentrates on playing the violin and performing music for the locals. Unsure where life will take her, a local performance got her noticed by the captain of a cruise ship and offered her a job she couldn't refuse.
  • Graham is the son of a rich property developer, and spends most his time anywhere his Father and Step-mother are not. His hobbies are reading lengthy books and keeping his cat, Spice, out of his father's hair. (What's left of it.)
